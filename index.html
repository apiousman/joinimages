How to Join or Merge Images Online for Free

Welcome to Join Images, the simplest tool to combine & merge photos & images into a single file. Whether you need to create a vertical collage for Pinterest, stitch screenshots for a tutorial, or merge photos for a comparison, our tool handles it instantly.

Why use this Image merging and joining tool?

  • 100% Private: Your photos are processed in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers.
  • No Watermarks: Download your merged images clean and free.
  • Smart Resizing: Automatically scale down larger images to fit perfectly with smaller ones without distortion.
  • Drag & Drop: Easily reorder your image stack before downloading.

How it works

  1. Click Add Images to select your files.
  2. Choose between Vertical (top-to-bottom) or Horizontal (side-by-side) stacking.
  3. Drag the thumbnails to rearrange the order.
  4. Click Download Merged Image to save your file.
